Welcome To

Carmel Rani Public School

Carmel Rani Public School is a co-educational institution owned and managed by the Jaya Matha Educational and Charitable Trust of Jaya Matha Province Pala, an indigenous religious congregation founded by St. Kuriakose Chavara. The school was established in 2003 and is affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi (Affiliation No: 931047). It features beautiful infrastructure, qualified and dedicated staff, and is recognized under Section 2(g) of the National Commission for Minority Educational Institutions Act, 2004. It is located in a rural area.
